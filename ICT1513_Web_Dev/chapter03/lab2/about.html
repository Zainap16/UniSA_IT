About Us

WWild Rescues is a registered nonprofit wildlife rescue and rehabilitation facility, located in Ocala, Florida. We help injured, sick, neglected, and orphaned wildlife. We help rehabilitate these animals and then release them back into the wild, if possible.

Wild Rescues is a member of the National Wildlife Rehabilitators Association.

We help many animals, such as:

  1. Raccoons
  2. Squirrels
  3. Fox
  4. Birds
  5. Horses
  6. Deer
  7. Pigs
  8. Reptiles
